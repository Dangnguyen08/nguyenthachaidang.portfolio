Harmony Heart2Heart

from member to president

08/2021 - Present

Harmony Heart2Heart's Description

  • The largest volunteer club in the Vinschool educational system was founded in 2019. People, the environment, and animals are the three key themes that Harmony Heart2Heart will focus on from 2019 to 2022.
  • Harmony Heart2Heart's colors and slogan, "Compassion to Action," have changed from 2022 to 2023. The organization now focuses on a different theme every year. Along with the three basic values—head, happiness, and heart—that the club members share with the community as well as with the general public. At Heart2Heart, we develop interpersonal relationships, support the neighborhood, and encourage one other's development of soft skills through events.

"Zalo Connect" Project

  • In Vietnam, a program called Zalo included a component titled "Supporting persons in need" during the Covid-19 outbreak. With the use of this application, Harmony Heart2Heart was able to carry out the "Zalo Connect" project to assist those who were struggling during the pandemic.
  • Harmony Heart2Heart Club was able to promptly carry out the project after only two days of fundraising thanks to excellent support and assistance. The club is overjoyed to have gained the benefactors' confidence and enthusiastic support. After the calling period, the club got 14,995,000 VND in total. Nearly 30 people/households who are having trouble because of the Covid-19 pandemic received aid totaling 13 million VND. The club will use the remaining funds for the upcoming charity round. The club will be openly honest about all expenditure decisions made based on the level of benefactor support on the Harmony Heart2Heart fanpage.

"Xom Than" Project

  • With the purpose of bringing a fulfilling Tet to the people living in Xom than, Harmony Heart2Heart’s “Xom than 2022” campaign is set out to raise fund toward these individuals by selling lì xì, tote bags and hosting auction at Vinschool the Harmony’s Tet Fair this spring - with 100% of the proceeds going directly to the patients at Xom than.
  • Raised more than 30 million to help households in "Xom Than" Dien Bien and Hanoi.

"Children of Cancer" Project

  • The "Children with Cancer" initiative of Harmony Heart2Heart was established and helped underprivileged kids at the National Children's Hospital with their basic needs.
  • After a month of working on the project, we are thrilled to report that we were able to provide approximately 50 underprivileged children with food and other essentials, like diapers, milk, and food. cash and miles. Harmony Heart2Heart was effective in getting supplies to kids via the National Children's Hospital despite several challenges with delivering supplies and communicating with kids.
