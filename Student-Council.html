Student Council's Description

The Vinschool Student Council Together with the Board of Directors and the Student Affairs Department, the Harmony High School, which was founded in 2019 to represent all students at the school, carries out publicity campaigns and plans events annual. The Vinschool student council has more than 50 students enrolled at Harmony High School.

I was selected as one of the three members of the Student Council's Advisory Board after serving on the Student Council for two years and earning the confidence of the Board of Directors and the Student Affairs Department. I am in charge of the organization's external and financial advisory section.

Prom Serenade'22: Deja Vu

A series of gala events are hosted yearly at Vinschool The Harmony High School, including Serenade '22: Déjà vu, a gala supper held at the conclusion of the 2021–2022 academic year. The event hopes to create a nostalgic environment of the 1990s with the theme "Step Back Into the 90s" by bringing back fond memories for all ages.

I have assisted in handling fundraising initiatives, working with partners and artists, and participating in the activities of the external affairs department. The amount of money that was successfully raised for the event exceeded 300 million.
